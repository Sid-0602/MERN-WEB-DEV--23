AI enabled Blind Stick for Visually impaired

Visually impaired people find it difficult to detect obstacles in front of them which makes it challenging to navigate in an unknown environment.
This impedes their physical movement.

Our proposal is a design of a smart stick equipped with IR and ultrasonic sensors to detect any obstacles in front of the user, within a range of four meters. PIR sensor is used to detect living entities in the surrounding. Speech warning messages are activated when an obstacle is detected

WEBIOT- IoT Based System for Waste Management

This Project aims to organize the collection of Street Waste by setting up trashcans in all public places.
The WEBIOT will aim to create an efficient system of processes at every level (collection, segregation, and treatment) in a way that will sustain itself in the future. The Project will be continuously involved in Research and Development in order to incorporate new ideas, creative solutions, and innovative thinking into our already established systems and processes. We are proposing an automated dustbin with the help of various sensors.

    Tech-Stack for the project:
  • Arduino IDE, Proteus for Circuit and Electronics part.
  • HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React for the front end of a responsive website.
  • Nodejs and Express Js for the backend of the website
  • Databases used are NoSql and MongoDB
